Comparison -> 1 Quantitative and 1 Qualitative -> Bar Chart
		Good for comparision
	Ex : Number of Sales(Quantitative) vs. Type of Fruit(Qualitative)
Trend -> Date and Quantitative variable -> line Chart
	Ex: Number of days per month or year
Correlation -> Temperature(Quantitative) and Spoiled Products -> Scatter plot
Preposition -> Total amount Sales by Category -> Pie chart
	Ex: % of Sales in different category
